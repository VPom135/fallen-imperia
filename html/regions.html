Regions

What remained after the fall.

After the fall of Empiria, the once unified lands fragmented into several distinct regions, each with its own unique characteristics and challenges. The fertile plains of Agreon, the scorched Island of Oteth, the vast Iublias Archipelago, and the far eastern lands of Riotao now stand as testaments to the empire's former glory and its subsequent decline.
