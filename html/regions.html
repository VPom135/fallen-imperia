What remained after the fall.

After the fall of Empiria, the once unified lands fragmented into several distinct regions, each with its own unique characteristics and ills. The sudden end of the imperial integration led to many shortages and disputes, the food producers found themselves only having old and broken tools, the mighty armies slowly starved, and the wealthy merchant routes were left to their own luck against criminals. As the central authority crumbled, local leaders and warlords rose to power, each seeking to fill the void left by the empire's collapse.
